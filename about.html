Technozion - The Annual Tech Fest of NIT Warangal

About Technozion

Technozion is the premier technical fest of the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Warangal, offering a platform for students to showcase their skills and engage in futuristic technological innovations. It's a 3-day event that brings together tech enthusiasts, professionals, and students to explore, innovate, and compete in exciting competitions and workshops.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to connect, learn, and grow. Through workshops, competitions, and tech talks, we aim to inspire innovation and foster creativity in the fields of technology. Our vision is to make Technozion a globally recognized tech fest that encourages young minds to think beyond the conventional and shape the future.
